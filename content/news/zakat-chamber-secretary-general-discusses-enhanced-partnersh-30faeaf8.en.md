[SNA] KHARTOUM, June 3, 2026 (SUNA) - Secretary-General of the Zakat Chamber Dr. Yahya Ahmed Abdullah Al-Qamrawi met on Wednesday in his office with Commissioner-General of the Social Safety, Solidarity and Poverty Reduction Commission Dr. Mohamed Ali to discuss ways of strengthening partnership and cooperation between the two sides in a number of programs and projects related to combating poverty and achieving social protection for targeted groups.
