Kandaka is an independent platform covering Sudan — its news, its history, and its future. We publish original analysis on Sudan's economy, infrastructure, and development, alongside a curated library of over 400 scholarly works, reports, and historical documents about the country.

Sudan is navigating one of the worst humanitarian crises in the world while sitting on vast agricultural, mineral, and water resources. Kandaka exists to document both realities and to think seriously about what comes next.

**[News](/news/)** — Daily coverage of Sudan from trusted sources, in English and Arabic.

**[Ideas](/ideas/)** — Original long-form analysis: solar energy, river transport, irrigation, industrialisation, and education.

**[Library](/library/)** — 413 documents: World Bank reports, archaeological studies, water resource papers, historical works, and more — all about Sudan.

**[Development](/development/)** — Key economic analyses and World Bank assessments of Sudan's economy.
